Solaris

2nd Year University Project

Solaris is a first person, pseudo-realistic exploration game set on the desert planet of Karnagor. The game's focus on art and storytelling helps guide the player through a mysterious, subterranean construction to unearth a shocking truth that threatens the reality of the people of Karnagor.

The Trial

3rd Year University Project

The Trial is a first Person Afrofuturistic parkour game with an emphasis on smooth, and fluid movement as well as engaging combat. The Trial uses a procedurally generated level to ensure endless replayability. The Trial adheres to culturally sensitive art and design.
